SwiftFillAI

SwiftFillAI is a Chrome extension that uses Google's Gemini AI to analyze and autofill financial forms in real time. It combines screen analysis, natural language processing, and a built-in chatbot to streamline financial workflows. Developed with JavaScript, Node.js, and Gemini API, SwiftFillAI focuses on automation, privacy, and user-friendly design.
