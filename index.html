StockStalker

StockStalker is a web-based stock market application designed to make investing approachable and intuitive. Whether you're just starting out or refining your strategy, StockStalker offers real-time and historical market data, personalized portfolio tracking, and paper trading to help users experiment without financial risk.

SwiftFillAI

SwiftFillAI is a Chrome extension that uses Google's Gemini AI to analyze and autofill financial forms in real time. It combines screen analysis, natural language processing, and a built-in chatbot to streamline financial workflows. Developed with JavaScript, Node.js, and Gemini API, SwiftFillAI focuses on automation, privacy, and user-friendly design.

EcoSnap

EcoSnap is an AI-powered sustainability social app that turns local observations into crowdsourced environmental research. Users document pollution and biodiversity with photos, helping convert everyday snapshots into verified data for researchers, city initiatives, and conservation programs.
